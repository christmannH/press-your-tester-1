How to Play PRESS YOUR TESTER

You are a Software Engineer looking to get paid BIG BUCKS for the code you wrote. But before you get paid, you have to PRESS YOUR TESTER!

First, you answer 3 multiple choice questions to prove your worth, each correct answer gives you 3 chances with the TESTER, each wrong answer will only get you 1 chance. This tester can find some NASTY BUGS in your code.

The goal is to make the most money you can without "bugging out" --> 3 BUGS in your code and you lose! You have a chance to deliver your code by pressing the STOP CODING button anytime and getting paid what you got so far.

GOOD LUCK!
